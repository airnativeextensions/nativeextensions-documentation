setIdentifier()method 
public function setIdentifier(value:String):DeviceConfiguration

Sets the identifier. The identifier will depend on the device and what may be used to specify the correct device.

  • Ingenico - The format of the Ingenico device identifier is "xxx-yyyyyyyy", where "xxx" is the device type and "yyyyyyyy" is the last 8 digits of the serial number found on the label on the back of the device. See examples of the currently supported Bluetooth devices below:
    • iCMP - iCM122-12345678
    • iSMP4 - iSMP4-12345678
