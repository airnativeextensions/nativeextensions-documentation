Classpublic class SubscriptionPeriod
InheritanceSubscriptionPeriod Object

A subscription period is a duration of time defined as some number of units, where a unit can be a UNIT_DAY, UNIT_WEEK, UNIT_MONTH, or UNIT_YEAR. For example, a subscription period of two weeks has a unit of UNIT_WEEK, and a numberOfUnits equal to 2.
