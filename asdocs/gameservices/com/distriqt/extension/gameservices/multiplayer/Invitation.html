Packagecom.distriqt.extension.gameservices.multiplayer
Classpublic class Invitation
InheritanceInvitation Object

This class represents an invitation to play a match.

A Player will receive an invitation whenever another player creates a match and selects them as a participant in the game. The player can then either, accept, decline or dismiss an invitation to a game.
