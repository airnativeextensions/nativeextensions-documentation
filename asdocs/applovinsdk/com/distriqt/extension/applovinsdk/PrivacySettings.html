doNotSell:Boolean

Publishers may choose to display a "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link. Such publishers may choose to set a flag indicating whether a user located in California, USA has opted to not have their personal data sold. If the user has not opted out of the sale of their personal information, please set the following flag to false.

isAgeRestrictedUserproperty 
isAgeRestrictedUser:Boolean

iOS Apps in the "Kids" category or iOS Apps intended for children may not use the AppLovin SDK per App Store Review Guidelines 1.3 and 5.1.4. To ensure COPPA, GDPR, and Google Play policy compliance, you should indicate when a user is a child. If the user is known to be in an age-restricted category (i.e., under the age of 16) please set the following flag to true.
