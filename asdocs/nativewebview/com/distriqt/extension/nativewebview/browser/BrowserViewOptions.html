animationOutproperty 
public var animationOut:int = 1

Animation when the browser view is closed by the user. This can be interpreted at as the direction that your application will animate in and push out the browser view.

Generally you will want the opposite of the animationIn property eg if animationIn is SLIDE_BOTTOM then you will most likely want animationOut to be SLIDE_TOP.

Support:

  • Android (only when closed by the user i.e. not when using the close function)

primaryColourproperty 
public var primaryColour:int = 0xEEEEEE

The primary colour of the control bar in the browser view. Where possible this colour is used for the background colour of the control bar in the browser view. Android and iOS v10+ support this.

secondaryColourproperty 
public var secondaryColour:int = 0x000000

A secondary colour, used in various places.

iOS will use this colour as the text for the control bar (preferredControlTintColor on iOS 10 + and tintColor on lower versions)
